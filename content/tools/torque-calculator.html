Uses the short-form relation T = K·F·d with F = (preload %) × proof stress × tensile stress area. Proof stresses per ISO 898-1 (metric) and SAE J429 (imperial). K-factor dominates the result and varies with coating, lubrication and surface condition — always validate against the joint design, fastener manufacturer data, or a calibrated tightening trial. Guidance only, not a substitute for engineering judgement.
